MY LIFE IN PHOTOGRAPHY

As a self tought photographer, I started my journey to become a business owner, selling my own photo art in 2014. Since then I've been selling my photos all over the world, to countries such as Iceland, Germany, Mexico and Australia. All of my photos are inspired by the scandinavian nature and the ever changing seasons.

Feel free to follow me on my journey in photography!
